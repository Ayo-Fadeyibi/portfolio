About Me

I'm a third-year Software Engineering student at the University of Queensland with a genuine passion for building things that work well and look great. My focus is on web development, from crafting responsive UIs to building the logic that drives them.

Moving from Nigeria to Australia four years ago wasn't just a change of location, it shaped how I approach problems. Adapting to a new environment built resilience, curiosity, and a drive to keep growing that shows up in how I approach my work as a developer.

Outside of study, I completed a software engineering internship at Fastlane IT Solutions where I shipped real features, squashed bugs under deadline pressure, and learned what it actually means to write code on a team. I'm now actively expanding into cloud (AWS) and deepening my data structures knowledge as I look toward my next internship.
